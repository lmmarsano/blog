Euclidean Algorithm

The Euclidean Algorithm is an iterative procedure for calculating the greatest common divisor of 2 integers. With a sequence starting with the 2 integers (the latter non-0), remainders of the previous 2 terms in the sequence are calculated until the last term is 0. The last non-0 term is the greatest common divisor.
